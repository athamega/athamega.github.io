Significance of He(-man)

In gematria, He symbolizes the number five

Attached to words, He may have three possible meanings:

  • A preposition meaning the definite article "the"
  • A prefix indicating that the sentence is a question. (For example, Ha'shem, (to me, it's a laugh about Eden, Isaac means "he laughs"))
  • A suffix after place names indicating movement towards the given noun. (For example, Adamah, towards Adam)
Did you know?
  • He is often used to represent the name of God, as He stands for Hashem, which means The Name.
